Sweet and Sour Pork

Description

A nice homemade recipe for porkchops baked in sweet and sour sauce, goes great with rice or mashed potatoes and your choice of vegitables.

This recipe makes enought sauce for 4 or 5 large porkchips but you can also substitute the pork for chicken breasts.

Ingredients

  • Meat: 4 or 5 porkchops thawed optionally trim fat to preference
  • Ketchup: 2 Cups Standard store bought Ketchup.
  • BBQ sauce: 2 - 4 Tbsp Original BBQ sauce more or less will be the main change to the flavor, vary amount or flavor to taste.
  • Vinegar: 1 Cup regular white vinegar.
  • Brown Sugar: 2 Cups Of your choice of yellow or golden brown sugar.
  • Mustard powder: 1 Tbsp regular mustard powder, havent tried standard mustard but I would assume yellow mustard would work as a substitute
  • Spices(Optional): I usually add some garlic powder and pepper. For a bit of spice some chili powder and cayenne powder works well but not enough to overwhelm.
  • Flour: Use a bit to thicken the sauce if desired.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375f
  2. Heat a pan to high and sear the porkchops, no need to cook them.
  3. Mix the sauce together and optionally use 1 or 2 tbsp flour to thicken as desired.
  4. Add seared meat to sauce in a baking dish ensure sauce gets both under and over the meat.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 45 min.
  6. Ensure meat is cooked to to desired doneness.
  7. Let dish stand uncovered for a few min before serving.
